CYBER SECURITY

Cybersecurity is a field dedicated to protecting digital systems, networks, and sensitive information from unauthorized access, data breaches, and cyber threats. It involves the development of strategies, technologies, and practices aimed at safeguarding computers, servers, mobile devices, and other digital assets from malicious attacks. In an increasingly interconnected world, where technology plays a vital role in our daily lives, cybersecurity is of paramount importance. It addresses the vulnerabilities and risks associated with computer systems and networks, aiming to mitigate potential threats and protect against unauthorized access, data theft, malware infections, and other cybercrimes. Cybersecurity professionals work to identify and assess potential security risks, develop and implement security measures, and respond to security incidents.
