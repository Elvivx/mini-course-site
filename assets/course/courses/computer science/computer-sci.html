COMPUTER SCIENCE

Computer Science is a field that encompasses the study of computation, algorithms, and the design of computer systems. It explores the theoretical foundations and practical applications of computing, ranging from developing software and algorithms to understanding the underlying hardware and network systems. At its core, computer science focuses on problem-solving, logical reasoning, and the manipulation and representation of information. It involves analyzing and designing efficient algorithms, developing software applications, and creating innovative technologies that drive advancements in various industries. Computer Science covers a wide range of topics, including data structures, programming languages, artificial intelligence, computer architecture, software engineering, databases, networking, and more.
